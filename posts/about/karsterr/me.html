Efe Can Kara: My IT Systems Journey

Posted by KARSTERR on May 28, 2024

Hello, everyone! My name is Efe Can Kara, a 21-year-old student at Sivas Cumhuriyet University, where I am currently pursuing a degree in IT Systems. I'm interested in data science and got passion about Linux. Today, I'd like to share a bit about my journey so far, my interests, and my goals for the future.

Early Education and Interests

I kicked off my school journey at Milas Cumhuriyet Elementary School, where my love for learning and tech first sparked. With my natural curiosity, I was always digging into new things, which still defines me today. Back then, I already knew my way around a computer and could handle my daily stuff or indulge in some gaming (mostly xD). Little did I know, that machine was about to shake up the world and totally change my life.

High School: Building the Foundation

When I first started high school at Milas Anatolian High School, I was so lost, dude. I didn't have many friends and I felt super lonely. But you know what? Those four years in high school totally changed me for the better. I met awesome people and formed really strong friendships. Even when things got tough and my grades started to slip, it was all good because of the support of my friends and a little bit of self-improvement. In the beginning, I was all about biology and wanted to be a genetic engineer, but then I lost interest.

I felt like I was going nowhere until 11th grade. And then a buddy of mine who was a total computer whiz introduced me to Linux. At first, I didn't think much of it, but when I had a computer problem, I figured, "Why not?" I started off with Fedora 33, and within a year, I was a Linux pro. I tried all kinds of operating systems, from Arch Linux and Gentoo to FreeBSD and its derivatives. That's when I decided to become a software developer. I was always into computers, but using Linux turned that interest into a full-on passion.

University Life: Diving into IT Systems

When I first stepped into uni, I was super excited but also kinda nervous about meeting new people. As it turns out, it was way cooler than I expected. The peeps in my department were all awesome. Some became like family, while others were just easy to get along with. And you know what? My bond with my high school buddies got even stronger, which always kept me grounded.

I really dug deep into stuff like algorithms, data structures, computer science, and databases here. I made sure I wrapped my head around these basics in class and through my own research. I even picked up programming languages like Python, C#, MySQL, and totally nailed those database models. It was a blast working on projects with them too. Even though I had a tough time picking a field as a programmer at first, eventually I shifted towards data science and aim to grow in this area, create projects, and level up big time.

Conclusion

Thus, hey there, I've shared a little peek into my life with you. I'm all about keeping my blog page lively and fun, so I'm really looking forward to sharing more about my experiences, thoughts, and ideas with all of you. Your feedback, thoughts, and even your constructive criticism are totally cool, so don't be shy! They help me get what you're into and how I can make things even better. Thanks a bunch for taking the time to read, and have an awesome day!
